About

The Global Career Carnival 2025 is a transformative educational summit designed to equip future professionals with the knowledge, resources, and mentorship needed to make informed career choices. Organized by the National Education & Charitable Trust (NECT) in collaboration with CIGI - Centre for Guidance India, this two-day event serves as a dynamic platform where students, educators, and industry leaders come together to explore a wide array of career opportunities. From emerging fields like AI, robotics, and data science to traditional career paths in civil services, finance, medicine, and media, the event provides deep insights into diverse industries, helping aspirants shape their professional journeys with confidence.

A key highlight of the event is the interactive career guidance sessions, featuring 25+ expert-led discussions and 60+ career booths, where attendees can directly engage with professionals, universities, and organizations. Industry pioneers, IAS officers, entrepreneurs, and subject-matter experts will share their experiences, offering valuable mentorship and real-world perspectives on career growth and future trends. Additionally, the A+ Achievers Honoring Ceremony will celebrate the academic excellence of top-performing SSLC and Plus Two students from Malappuram District, further encouraging young minds to strive for success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the Career Carnival Expo?

Bring multiple copies of your resume, a notepad, a pen, and a professional attitude. Some companies may also request a digital portfolio or LinkedIn profile.

Do I need to register in advance, or can I walk in?

Registration details vary, but most expos allow both pre-registration and walk-ins. Pre-registering may give you access to exclusive sessions or early job postings.

Will there be on-the-spot interviews?

Yes! Some companies conduct initial screenings or interviews on the spot, so dress professionally and be prepared with a quick elevator pitch about yourself.

Are there networking opportunities beyond job booths?

Absolutely! The expo may feature networking zones, panel discussions, and career workshops where you can connect with industry professionals.

Is the event only for job seekers, or can students and freshers attend?

The expo is open to everyone, including students, fresh graduates, and experienced professionals. It’s a great opportunity to explore industries, gain insights, and build connections.
